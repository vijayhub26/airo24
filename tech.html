ROUND 1

Berlin's Art Vision - GenVis

Description:

Berlin's Art Vision - GenVis challenges participants to harness the power of AI image generation platforms like DALL-E to recreate a given image with precision and creativity. It's a race to see who can prompt the AI most effectively to generate an image closest to the original. This event is a blend of art and technology, pushing the limits of AI-driven creativity. The most accurate and compelling rendition wins the prize!


Rules and Regulations:
  • Maximum of 2 participants per team, both from the same institution.
  • Each participant can join only one team.
  • An image will be provided at the start. Teams must use AI platforms like DALL-E to generate a similar image.
  • Submit the final generated image within the specified timeframe of Round 1.
  • Only AI image generation platforms like DALL-E are allowed. Manual edits outside these tools are not permitted.
  • Entries will be judged on how closely they match the provided image in terms of detail, style, and accuracy.
  • All prompts and images must be created during the event. Pre-generated content will lead to disqualification.
  • AThe team with the most accurate and creative image wins exciting prizes. Bring your AI skills to the forefront and create stunning visuals in Berlin's Art Vision - GenVis!

ROUND 2

Cryptic Heist

Description:

Cryptic Heist is a word-guessing game with a twist. Your mission is to guess a secret word by making guesses that are related in meaning. Each guess gives you hints, bringing you closer to the target word. It's a race against time that tests your language skills and strategy. Join the heist, crack the code, and prove you're the master of words!


Rules and Regulations:
  • Teams can have up to 2 members.
  • Guess the secret word using related words.
  • Make guesses and get hints on how close you are to the secret word.
  • The game ends when a team guesses the secret word. The winner is the team with the fewest guesses. If it's a tie, the team with the closest guesses wins.
  • You have 15 minutes to guess the word. If time runs out, the closest guesses will be reviewed to find the winner. Get ready to crack the code and become the master of the Cryptic Heist!
