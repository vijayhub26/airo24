The Professor's Blueprint - Paper Presentation

Description:

Step into the spotlight and showcase your research on cutting-edge topics like Generative AI, Data Analytics, Robotics and Automation, and more. "The Professor's Blueprint" is your chance to present groundbreaking ideas, engage with experts, and contribute to the future of technology. Whether it's the ethics of AI or the potential of AR/VR, this is the platform to make your voice heard in the AI and data science community.

Topics:
  • Generative AI
  • Data Analytics
  • Network Security
  • Ethics in AI
  • Robotics and Automation
  • AR/VR

Rules and Regulations:
  • Teams may include a maximum of 2 participants.
  • Both team members must be from the same college.
  • PPT Slide Limit Should be in Min 6 - 12 slides.
  • Each team is allotted 5 minutes for their presentation and 2 minutes forQ&A.
  • No individual may participate in more than one team.
  • Winners will be awarded with cash price.
  • Paper must adhere to the above specified topics.
