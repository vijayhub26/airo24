ABOUT AIRO


AIRO'24 is a premier National Level Technical Symposium organised by the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Velalar College of Engineering and Technology. This symposium brings together the brightest minds, fostering an environment of innovation and knowledge exchange in the rapidly evolving fields of AI and Data Science. AIRO'24 promises an exciting lineup of events, workshops, and competitions designed to inspire, challenge, and showcase cutting-edge advancements. Join us at AIRO'24 to explore groundbreaking ideas, network with experts, and be a part of the future of technology!
