The Escape Plan


ROUND 1

Plan Chenobyl

Description:

Challenge

  • Participants will briefly view a large set of images and then recall and describe their content. This test evaluates their visual memory and descriptive skills.
  • Participants will receive a given word and must create as many new words as possible using only the letters from the original word within a time limit. This challenge tests their creativity and linguistic agility.

ROUND 2

Red Mask Challenge

Description:

In the second round, participants will engage in an XOX (Tic-Tac-Toe) game, where strategic thinking and planning are key. Following the XOX game, they will tackle a cup-flipping challenge. This combined challenge tests both their strategic and physical coordination skills.


ROUND 3

The Heist Vault

Description:

Gear up for "The Heist Vault - Mystery Hunt," where your mission is to uncover hidden treasure on campus. Crack codes, solve clues, and outsmart challenges in this high-stakes, adrenaline-fueled race. Only the sharpest will unlock the vault and claim the prize. The clock is ticking—are you ready to pull off the ultimate heist?


Rules and Regulations:
  • Participants must register in advance and be on time for each round.
  • Each round has strict time limits; no extensions will be granted.
  • Use of external aids (notes, devices) and outside help is prohibited.
  • Adherence to honesty and integrity is mandatory; cheating results in disqualification.
  • Participants must respect and follow the instructions of event moderators; their decisions are final.
